ELECTRONICS & COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

Electronics & Communication along with computer engineering has made this world flat. The importance of the subject is more evident today in the evolution of consumer and industrial electronics that has undergone a sea-change. The Department is pursuing research in Video object detection and tracking. Visual Surveillance, Image Processing and Analysis. Adaptive Signal Processing, Biomedical Signal and Image Processing, Channel Equalization, Mobile Computing and Smart Antenna Systems and VLSI Design. The students are trend to understand the functions of devices like diodes, BJTs, FETs, OPAMPs and Digital gates, Principal instruments are CROs, Function Generators and Regulated Power Supply. The Analog and Digital Electronics Circuit Laboratories inlcude circuit simulation packages like Multisim, Tanner tools and P-Spice. The signal processing laboratories have advanced software like Matlab (version 7.14) with toolboxes for image processing, fuzzy logic, genetic algorithm and signal processing. Digital Electronics Circuits Laboratory uses simulation languages like Verilog and VHDL. Principal equipments are CROs, Function Generators, Regulated Power Supply, Digital Multimeter. The Microprocessor and Microcontroller Laboratory houses hardware and software like Keil PK 51, Keil MCB 2919-ED, Reil MCBST M32E-ED, Keiliu MDK-ARM. The VLSI laboratory has 60 floating license of Complete Tanner Tools Pro to facilitate VLSI design. Communication Engineering Laboratory is equipped with DSOs, CROs, Function Generators and Communication Trainer Kits. Communication System Lab deals with Microwave, Optical and Satellite communication systems with equipments Microwave Benches, Fiber Optics Trainers, Satellite Trainer, Spectrum Analyzer and Trainer Kits. Digital Signal Processing Laboratory includes equipments like MATLAB 7.0.1, TMS 6713 DSP Processors with Code Composer studio. Project Laboratory is equipped with MATLAB, Tanner, Com-Sim software and lab view. The M.Tech Laboratory uses equipments like DSP processor like TMS 6713, MSP 430 and different Data Acquisition card.
