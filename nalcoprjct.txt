 <h4><font color="white" size="5">The Institute of Technical Education and Research is the Engineering School of the Siksha O Anusandhan University situated in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India. This institute was established in the year 1996 by Dr. Manoj Ranjan Nayak. Institute of Technical Education and Research (ITER) was established in the year 1996 in Bhubaneswar and it received AICTE approval for Undergraduate degree (B.Tech) courses. Initially it was affiliated to Utkal University and from 2002 to 2007, it was affiliated to Biju Patnaik University of Technology (BPUT). Since 2007, Institute of Technical Education and Research (ITER), Bhubaneswar is a constitute institute of Siksha O Anusandhan University. The institute campus covers 46 acres. Students are admitted into this College through Siksha O Anusandhan University Admission Test (SAAT). ITER, Siksha O Anusandhan University is considered as a par with finest Institutes like Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur and Jadavpur University in the eastern part of India in terms of curricula.</font></h4>
